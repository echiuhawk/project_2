Background

  • William Jones College Preparatory High School is the full name
  • Jones is locatd in Printer's Row in Chicago, Illinois
  • This school is a selective enrollment school

Floors at the South Building at JCP

  1. First Floor: The first floor includes the lobby, the first floor of the auditorium, and the main office as well as the link that connects the new building to the old building
  2. Second Floor: The second floor includes the cafeteria and the second floor balcony of the auditorium
  3. Third Floor: The library and music rooms are located here
  4. Fourth Floor: The sculpture room and other classrooms including math and english are here
  5. Fifth Floor: Math, science, and english rooms are here as well as some special classrooms
  6. Sixth Floor: Physical education rooms and a gym as well as lockers for men and women
  7. Seventh Floor: Locker room, swimming pool, gym, and balcony
